About Us

The IEEE (MTT-S) Chapter at NIT Jamshedpur is dedicated to advancing innovation and excellence in antennas and propagation for the benefit of humanity. We promote research, knowledge sharing, and professional development among our members.

Our chapter is part of the prestigious IEEE organization and operates within the Kolkata Section of Region 10. We organize technical talks, workshops, and seminars throughout the year to keep our members updated with the latest advancements in the field.
